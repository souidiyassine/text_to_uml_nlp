Each hotel contains a number of rooms, each room is rented by a fixed price. The manager of the hotel manages the rental of the rooms.
Access to the bathrooms is free since it is included in the price of the rooms. Some rooms do not have a bathroom. The other rooms, such as kitchen, reception hall, are not part of the study. People can rent one or more rooms in the hotel. In other words: the hotel accommodates a certain number of people.
The reservation of a room is made by a customer, the manager looks for a free room, if there is the manager gives the price of the room to the customer who becomes the tenant of the room after having paid. Otherwise, the customer leaves.
